  • Glycolysis - step by step

        a. Overall reaction:  Glucose + 2 NAD+ + 2 ADP + 2 Pi  -->  2 Pyruvate + 2 NADH + 2 H+ + 2 ATP

        b. Step by step reaction (10 steps):

            1: phosphorylation (regulated)

            2. isomerization

                    -- phosphohexose isomerase mechanism

            3. second phosphorylation  (regulated)

            4. aldol cleavage of F-1,6-bP

                    -- aldolases mechanism

            5. triose phosphate interconversion

                   -- glucose carbons in GAP

            6. oxidation of GAP 

                    -- mechanism of GAPDH

            7. production of ATP  (Substrate-level phosphorylation to make ATP)

            8. migration of phosphate

                    -- phosphoglycerate mutase mechanism

            9. dehydration of 2-PG to PEP

            10. second production of ATP  (regulated) (Substrate-level phosphorylation to make ATP)

                    -- pyruvate tautomerization
