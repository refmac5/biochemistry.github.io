Protein Analysis

SDS-PAGE

- protein molecules are in reducing and denatured condition

- Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis (PAGE)

- based on the sizes of protein molecules


Native PAGE

- protein molecules are in non-reducing and non-denaturing condition

- separation is based on charge to mass ratio of protein molecules

- secondary structure and native charge density of protein are maintained


Western Blot Transfer

- transfer SDS-PAGE onto membrane

- separation based on specific antibody binding

- confirm the identity of the protein molecule

- antigen antibody interaction

- example


Iso Electric Focusing (IEF)

- isoelectric point

- separation based on charges of proteins

- confirm the homogeneity of the protein
